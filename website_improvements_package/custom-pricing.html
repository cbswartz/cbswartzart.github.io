The Commission Process

  1. Initial Consultation: We'll discuss your vision, reference photos, and any specific details you'd like included.
  2. Proposal: I'll provide a detailed quote based on your requirements.
  3. Deposit: A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your commission.
  4. Sketch Approval: I'll send a preliminary sketch for your review and approval.
  5. Creation: I'll create your portrait with meticulous attention to detail.
  6. Final Approval: You'll receive a digital proof of the completed portrait for final approval.
  7. Delivery: Upon final payment, your portrait will be carefully packaged and shipped to you.
