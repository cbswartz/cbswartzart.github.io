Frequently Asked Questions

How do I commission a custom portrait?

Simply fill out the contact form above or email me directly. We'll discuss your vision, reference photos, and any specific details you'd like included. Once we agree on the details, I'll provide a quote and timeline.

What information do you need for a commission?

High-quality reference photos are essential. The clearer and more detailed the photos, the better the final portrait will be. I'll also need to know your preferred size, timeline, and any specific elements you'd like me to focus on.

How long does a commission take?

Depending on the complexity and my current workload, commissions typically take 2-6 weeks to complete. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee, subject to availability.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. International shipping costs will be calculated based on your location and the size of the artwork.
